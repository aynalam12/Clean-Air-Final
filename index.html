Our Mission

Our mission is to keep our air as clean as possible by creating solutions to current pollution issues and spreading awareness of the impact of climate change and global warming on population health. We hope to reverse the harm caused by air pollution to help communities reduce their carbon footprint.

Meet the Team

Jane Thomas

CEO & Founder

Jane Thomas started off her journey as a young Environmental Manager in Arizona where she was responsible for the environmental business and led a multidisciplinary group of professionals that delivered environmental projects for federal and industrial clients. As much as she adored her job, she yearned for something more passionate. Global Warming has always been something she worried about since childhood and it inspired her determination to lead something for change; create her own team and community of people with the same passions to strive for change in the environment. Despite already graduating Arizona State University with a master's degree in environmental sciences, Thomas decided to go back to school to study business management so she could become a CEO everyone could be proud of.

JaneThomas@airpurification.com

Mike Ross

Director of Waste Division Programs

Ross is the most recent member of the team. He graduated from Manipal Institute of Technology with a master's degree in Environmental Engineering and Civil Engineering which he double majored in. Already knowing where he wanted to use his talents for, he searched high and low for a good fitting job. One morning, he ran into the CEO of the organization at a cafe and they fell into conversation where she told him all about her mission and passion for change. After announcing his interest and passing the interview days later, he was able top begin directing the division overseas that cleanup at state and superfund sites, permits and inspeacts hazardest and solid waste activities and facilities, and promotes waste reduction through pollution prevention and recycling.

Mross@airpurification.com

Lilianna Edwards

Deputy Director

If you had told teenage Lilianna that she would become a deputy director of an organization based around air purification, she would of told you that there is nothing else she could ever be in this world other than a veterinarian. If it wasn't for her youngest sister, Mya, telling her that she could see her being a natural scientist she would of never ended up switching her major from animal scienes to environmental science with a minor in business. Now, after graduating from Westchester University, she couldn't be happier managing and overseeing all the operations this organization has to offer.

LiliE@airpurification.com

Jonothan Perez

Director of Air Quality

Jonothan perez is the first Latin American to ever be on the team which he takes great pride in. He also takes pride in his role as a director for air quality, his job requiring him to protect public health and the environment by helping control future and present air pollution sources. Perez graduated from University of California, Berkely with a bachelor's in Environmental Engineering.
